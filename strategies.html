Strategies

The American Citizens for Justice (ACJ) used multiple strategies to get justice for Vincent Chin, from coalition building and solidarity to publicity and legal tactics. These allowed the ACJ to have a multi-pronged strategy to leverage different forms of power. Coalition building, solidarity, and use of publicity and media allowed the ACJ to gain larger numbers of supporters. Many Americans were not even aware of the injustice Chin faced, so the publicity and coalition building was critical for mobilizing and organizing support for the movement. Justice for Vincent Chin could be achieved by punishing his killers through the legal justice system. The three-pronged legal strategy allowed for multiple tactics to use to fight a difficult legal hurdle.
